# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct (v2.1).

- Our Pledge: Foster an open and welcoming environment.
- Our Standards: Be respectful and constructive; harassment and exclusionary behavior are not tolerated.
- Our Responsibilities: Maintainers may remove or reject contributions not aligned with this policy.

For full text, see: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct

To report unacceptable behavior, please email the maintainers at: security@your-domain.example
